One-Time Donation

We are most grateful for your giving to Lightning & Thunder Ministry. Your gift will help send the gospel throughout the earth (Acts 2:8). Jesus told His disciples in Matthew 9:37 & 38, “ The harvest truly is plentiful, but the laborers are few.” Therefore pray the Lord of the harvest to send labors into the harvest.

As we go in the power and anointing of the Holy Spirit, you go with us. By helping send us to the nations, you are a part of that prophetic anointing and flow to see souls saved from the kingdom of darkness-snatched from the devils hand and placed into the Kingdom of God. In that anointing the captives are set free and the sick are healed.

Become a Monthly Partner

As you partner with Lightning & Thunder Ministry you are making it possible for the message of Jesus Christ to be preached in 3rd world countries like Zambia, Congo, Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda, and India.

Your loving support enables Lightning & Thunder Ministry to:
  • Plants churches
  • Trains pastors in the LTM Supernatural Training Center
  • Feed children
  • Ministers to the orphans and the widows
  • Assist other ministries
  • Support Christian based school
  • Materials to train pastors such as bicycles and Bibles

As a partner with Lightning & Thunder Ministry you share in the anointing, rewards and fruits from the salvation, healing and deliverance of the oppressed. We pray for the anointing and blessings of God’s Spirit to overtake you as you reap the rewards of your generosity. We ask that Father God would open up the heavens for you to receive the spirit of increase of wisdom, revelation and financial blessings.
