Cuba Update

Revival Harvest Center

Lightning and Thunder Ministry has purchased property in Camaguey, Cuba for the purpose of advancing the Kingdom of God. The Cuban people are extremely excited and are meeting under the trees with the vision of a Revival Harvest Center to be built.

There is a three bedroom, two bathroom ministry house on the land to be completely restored for the pastors. Also a school across the street with many children. The area is prime for evangelism. There are no churches in this section of Camaguey.

This wonderful 4 acre property already has water, sewer, two wells. There are many different kinds of fruit trees to help supply the people with food.

We have excellent contractors and workers that are volunteering their labor on this project of clearing the land, building the Revival Harvest Center as well as remodeling the ministry house.

If you would like to give to this God appointed mission effort, all donations go directly for the purchasing of materials.
