Prophetic Words for 2013

by Prophetic Revivalist Richard Kessler on Jan. 6th 2013

  1. It is a time to dream bigger, God has a large place for you. The Lord is going to resurrect dead dreams. Ps. 34:3&4
  2. God filling spiritual territories. A year of mountains being removed. A year of deliverances. Being filled with the fullness of Christ. Eph. 3:19; Col. 2:7-10
  3. A year of speaking the language of heaven. There will be acceleration of manifestation of the words that we speak. Mark 11: 23-24; Rev. 2:16
  4. We are a double portion generation. Is. 61:7
  5. Miracles are in Motion for you. It is time to do different; some will touch God this year like never before. Matt. 14:35,36
  6. he flesh of the Middle East will have no peace. John 16:33
  7. The Lord says, “My church is not prepared for what is coming”. Rom 13:11; Matt. 25: 8-13
  8. A year to arise and go forward, that the Kingdom of Heaven becomes our present reality. Matt. 10:7-8; Ps.8:4-6; 2Cor.5:7
  9. A season to increase our time of separation with God. John 3:30
