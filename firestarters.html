November 24th - Ottumwa, Iowa  at the Bridgeveiw Center

FIRESTARTERS has been traveling throughout Minnesota and the mid west for several years, calling this generation to pursue the Lord with passion and purity.

God is pouring out on His Body a level of creativity that we have not seen since the Renaissance. Blending anointed musicianship with extravagant worship and the arts, Firestarters is a model of a new sound and a new way.

Our desire is to see a reformation within the church, with love being the driving force behind this second Jesus people movement. We are calling fathers and sons, mothers and daughters, the young and the old to come together in worship. This love revolution is our only hope in radically impacting our state, nation and world.
